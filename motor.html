Car Insurance

What is Car Insurance?

Car insurance, your trusty shield on the road, protects you and your vehicle from financial burdens arising from various unforeseen circumstances. It covers expenses related to damages and losses caused by accidents, thefts, natural disasters, and even liabilities towards third parties. Whether you prioritize legal compliance with a basic third-party plan or seek ultimate peace of mind with comprehensive coverage, PremiumBima offers a diverse range of options tailored to your unique needs and budget.

What's Covered?

  • Accidents:Protection against collision and repair costs.
  • Theft:Compensation for your stolen vehicle or its market value.
  • Fire:Coverage for fire damage to your car.
  • Natural Disasters:Protection against losses caused by floods, cyclones, etc.
  • Personal Accident:Coverage for death or disability in case of a car accident.
  • Third-Party Liabilities:Financial protection for damages caused to others (vehicles, property, or individuals).

Boost Your Protection with Optional Add-Ons:

  • Zero Depreciation Cover: Eliminates depreciation deductions during repairs, providing the full value for replacements.
  • Return to Invoice Cover: Receives the full invoice value (including registration and road tax) in case of theft or irreparable damage.
  • Tyre Protect Cover: Safeguards against unexpected tire issues (bursts, bulges, cuts).
  • Breakdown Assistance: Get convenient 24/7 help during car breakdowns anywhere.
  • Consumable Cover: Get reimbursed for essential replacements like engine oil, lubricants, etc., after accidents.
  • Engine and Gearbox Protection: Comprehensive protection for these vital components beyond standard coverage.
  • Daily Conveyance Benefit: Receive compensation for daily transportation while your car is repaired.
  • Key and Lock Protect: Covers replacement costs for damaged car locks due to theft, loss, or damage.
  • Loss to Personal Belongings: Reimbursement for personal items within the car during a covered incident.
  • Pay as You Drive Cover: Enjoy premium discounts based on your actual driving mileage.

What's Not Covered?

  • Damages to your car under a third-party policy.
  • Driving under the influence or without a valid license.
  • Driving with an unlicensed driver (except for learners with valid license holders).
  • Consequential damages arising indirectly from accidents.
  • Losses due to negligence (e.g., driving a car in floods).
  • Situations not covered by specific add-ons.

Frequently Asked Questions

➢ Is car insurance mandatory in India?

Yes, having at least a third-party car insurance policy is mandatory as per the Motor Vehicles Act.

➢ What documents do I need to buy car insurance from PremiumBima?

You'll need basic documents like your:

  • Driver's license
  • Registration certificate (RC) of your car
  • Previous year's insurance policy document (if applicable)
  • Address proof (ID card, Aadhaar card, etc.)

➢ Can I customize my car insurance plan with PremiumBima?

Absolutely! We offer multiple plans and 10 optional add-ons to personalize your coverage based on your needs and budget.

➢ What is the claim process like with PremiumBima?

Our claim process is designed to be quick and hassle-free. You can file a claim online or through our mobile app, upload pictures of the damage, and track the progress in real-time. We have a wide network of cashless garages for convenient repairs.

➢ How long does it take to get my claim settled with PremiumBima?

We strive to settle claims as quickly as possible. We have a high claim settlement ratio, and most claims are resolved within 7 days.

➢ What happens if I don't file a claim for several years?

You'll be eligible for a No Claim Bonus (NCB), which translates to discounted premiums on your car insurance renewal. The longer you go without claiming, the higher your NCB.

➢ What are the different types of car insurance policies offered by PremiumBima?

We offer three main types of car insurance:

  • Third-Party Liability: Covers damage to third-party vehicles, property, and individuals.
  • Comprehensive: Covers both third-party and own damage to your car.
  • Own Damage: Covers only damage to your own car.

➢ Can I choose my own repair garage with PremiumBima?

Yes, you can choose any authorized repair garage, but you may benefit from our cashless garage network for a more convenient experience.

➢ What happens if my car is stolen?

If your car is stolen and covered under your policy, we will compensate you based on the insured value of your car.

➢ Do I need to renew my car insurance every year?

Yes, car insurance policies are valid for one year only and need to be renewed before they expire to maintain coverage.

➢ What are the benefits of using PremiumBima's online platform?

Our online platform allows you to easily buy, renew, and manage your car insurance policy, file claims, and track their progress, all from the comfort of your home.

➢ What are the payment options available for buying car insurance from PremiumBima?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, net banking, and wallet payments.

➢ Can I get a discount on my car insurance premium?

Yes, you can get discounts on your premium based on factors like your NCB, your car's safety features, and your chosen deductible amount.

➢ What is the difference between IDV and market value of my car?

IDV (Insured Declared Value) is the maximum amount the insurance company will pay for your car in case of total loss or theft. It is usually less than the market value of your car.

➢ What is deductible and how does it affect my car insurance premium?

Deductible is the amount you agree to pay towards repairs before the insurance company covers the rest. Choosing a higher deductible can lower your premium, but you'll need to pay more out of pocket if you file a claim.
